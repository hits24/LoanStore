Documents Required

Home Loan

  1. ID Proof (PAN, Aathar, Voter ID, etc..)
  2. Address Proof (Ration Card, Aadhar Card, Rent Agreement, Gas Bill, etc..)
  3. Pay Slips/ Salary Certificate (For Cash Salary Profiles)
  4. Udyam Registration/ SSI Certificate (For Self Employed Profiles)
  5. Bank Statement (Latest 6 months)

Business Loan

  1. ID/ Address Proof of Promoters
  2. Vintage Proof ( GST/Udyam Certificate)
  3. Latest 3 months GSTR 3B.
  4. Latest 12 months Bank Statement
  5. Own House Proof( EB Receipt/ Property Tax Receipt)

Personal Loan

  1. ID/ Address Proof( PAN, Aathar, Voter ID, etc..)
  2. Employee ID Card
  3. Last 3 months Pay Slip
  4. Last 6 months Bank Statement
  5. Last 3 years ITR and 2 years Business Vintage proof required for Self Employed Profiles
